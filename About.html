About Wave Jewelry

This entire business is inspired by the wave of the ocean. The goal is to raise awareness and to raise money to help the sea stay clean, and for it to be a healthy environment for all marine life to live in.

How Your Purchase Helps

15% of all proceeds go towards research and supplies to help make the oceans safer and to prevent marine life from going extinct. The money being gives scientists and other researchers more opportunities to make breakthroughs and important findings on how to make our oceans a safe place for the ecosystems that live within them.

Location

Located by the sea for optimal convince to gather materials and to appreciate what nature has to offer.
